☠Rowlett☠

Rowlett is a city in Dallas and Rockwall counties in the U.S. state of Texas, and a northeastern suburb of Dallas. The population was 56,199 as of the 2010 census. It is a quiet, upscale bedroom community in the Dallas to Fort Worth metroplex, located on Lake Ray Hubbard.

Just 20 minutes from downtown Dallas north of IH-30, surrounded by more than 30 miles of shoreline on beautiful Lake Ray Hubbard.
