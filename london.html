♚London♚

London is the capital city of England. It is the most populous city in the United Kingdom, with a metropolitan area of over 13 million inhabitants.

London has a population of 8.539 (2014). London has the highest population density in Britain. London is the seat of central government in Britain. London was the first city in the world to have an underground railway, known as the 'Tube'.
